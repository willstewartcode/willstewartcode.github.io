 About

I am a front-end web developer and designer from Cleveland, TN. I studied graphic design at Lee University from 2018-2020 before transitioning to web development and mobile app development at Austin Peay State University from 2020-2023. Prior to desiging websites, I focused on logo design and social media graphics using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I also learned about and practiced photography as a hobby.

As a web designer, I am focused on the UI/UX design of websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and WordPress. I strive to make all my web designs responsive by using the latest features available in these technologies.
